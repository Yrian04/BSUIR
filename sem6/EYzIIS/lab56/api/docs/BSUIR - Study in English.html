Study in English

BSUIR was the 1st engineering university in the Republic of Belarus to open English-medium study programmes. In 2009, the first group of students was enrolled in the programme Telecommunication Networks. 2010 saw the first intake to an English-medium Master's degree programme Methods and Systems of Information Protection, Information Security.

Today, every 3rd foreign student at BSUIR studies in English. In compliance with the national system of higher education BSUIR offers four full-time and two distance first degree programmes and four full-time Master's degree programmes with English as the course language.

In addition, BSUIR offers blended programmes that combine full-time and e-learning in the English language that have been developed in cooperation with the Computer Systems Institute (CSI), the USA. Training is provided for the US residents on the basis of two BSUIR branches opened on the CSI campuses in Boston and Chicago.

BENEFITS & ADVANTAGES
• Join the elite group of students trained in English
• Have an interview in English at the university. Neither a IELTS nor a TOEFL certificate is required
• Get an internationally recognised diploma within a four-year period
• Improve your competitiveness in the international labour market
• Keep your English up-to-date and learn specialised terminology without taking extra classes or courses
• Join a multicultural campus, learn more about different cultures and traditions
• Enjoy training in small groups
• Gain practical skills in the world's top IT companies
• Live in a super comfortable student dormitory

FIRST DEGREE PROGRAMMES IN ENGLISH
INFOCOMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGIES
Faculty of information security, Major in Infocommunication Networks
Qualification - Infocommunication Engineer

The Programme is focused on technologies of transport networks, access networks, channel and package digital commutation, as well as various network services, related to information transfer, storage, processing and distribution of information in communication networks. More

In the course of integration of media and communication means, Integrated Services Digital Networks (ISDN), Intelligent Networks (IN), Family Support Networks (FSN) were developed. Modern infocommunication systems are based on packet switching nodes, enabling information distribution in transport nets, as well as access of stationary and mobile subscribers to various types of services, related to data and media (voice, sound, image, video) transfer.

Various procedures for coding, filtration, compression and encryption of text, voice, sound and image are used to support multimedia technologies in modern devices and information distribution systems.

Specialised courses:
• Basics of Teletraffic Theory, and Telecommunication Networks and Systems
• Systems of Mobile Radio Communication
• Digital Data Transfer Systems
• Digital Processing of Voice and Images
• Theory of Coding

Within this programme an additional study of cryptographical information protection, code-division multiplex mobile systems, geocommunication technologies and communication alarm systems is available.

Graduates obtain proper knowledge and skills to work in research, design and maintenance of infocommunication networks and systems.

Faculty of information security
Qualification - Specialist in Information Security, Telecommunication Engineer

Active development and updating of information security technologies such as electronic digital signatures, ID infrastructure, network protocols protection, antivirus protection and content filtering are drastically changing the telecommunications sphere. More

Services which are mainly engaged in security protection are evolving in enterprises, institutions and offices. The improvement of electronic commerce and modern payment systems highlights the importance of training personnel in information security.

Specialised courses:

• Cryptographical Security of Information
• Digital Transmission Systems
• Commutation Systems
• Secure Data Transfer Systems
• Biometric Systems of Access Control in Telecommunication Networks

AUTOMATED DATA PROCESSING SYSTEMS
Faculty of Computer Systems and Networks
Qualification - Information Technologies Engineer

The principal concept of the programme combines the basic ideas of system analysis, algorithm design, programming technologies with a special focus on databases and network computing. This programme embraces both information technologies and control. More

Students learn the details of data collection, transmission, storing, processing, transformation and representation in the up-to-date computing systems alongside methods and techniques of design, development, realisation, installation and support of automated information systems.

Specialised courses:
• Operating Systems (Windows Vista/ Windows XP/Windows 2008/10 Server/NT)
• Programming Languages (Assembler WIN 32, MS Visual C++, MS Visual Basic, Borland Delphi, GPSS World)
• Databases and Case Technologies (MS Access, VS Visual FoxPro 9.0, MS SQL Server 2008/2010, MySQL, BPWin, ErWin)
• Electronic Documentation Systems (Lotus Notes, LanDocs)
• Integrated Manufacturing Software and Visual Frameworks (.NET, NetBeans)
• Applied to Internet Programming (C#.NET, Java, PHP, ASP.NET, JavaScript)

Students are trained to solve problems in automated data processing and control systems, including system formalisation and goal-making, by using analytical approaches to study system behavior and developing computational algorithms and corresponding software. These skills are used to develop corporative control, management and information systems, Internet applications (interactive web-sites and web-portals), IT-penetration in government economic projects and etc.

ENGINEERING-PSYCHOLOGICAL MAINTENANCE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGIES
Faculty of Computer-Aided Design
Qualification - Computer Engineer

This is an interdisciplinary programme majoring both in IT and Human Science. Human engineering is aimed to optimise IT product performance by taking into consideration human physical and cognitive capabilities and limitations into consideration during the design. More

Effective utilisation of newly developed software and hardware depends greatly on the fact, whether 'human factor' was taken into account in their design and operation. Data organisation and data input-output hardware usually do not take into account psychological human features of data receipt, processing, storage and realisation. Nowadays, specialists in 'human factor engineering' are necessarily included in work teams involved in design and operation of IT facilities. These specialists are deeply experienced in computer science, as well as in psychology, industrial design, statistics, operations research and anthropometry.

Specialised courses:
• Basics of Algorithmisation and Programming
• Descriptive Geometry and Engineering Graphics
• Anatomy and Physiology of Central Nervous System
• Physiology of Higher Nervous Function and Sensor Systems
• Operation Systems and Database Management
• Metrology, Standardisation and Certification in Informatics and Radioelectronics
• Functional Electronics and Microsystem Technology
• Basics of Discrete Mathematics
• Psychology of Information Perception and Processing
• Ergonomic Management of Information Systems and Technologies
• Design Technology for Intellectual Systems
• Usability Testing
• Programming languages and technologies (C/C++, PHP, CSS, HTML/XHML, Java, Javascript, HTTP, FTP)

Graduates of this programme are capable of:
• System Analysis, Estimation of Human Engineering Requirements in Information and Technical Systems
• Development, Installment, Testing and Maintenance of System Software and Applied Software
• Design of Databases and Technical Units of Data Processing Complexes
• Administration of Database Systems, Local and Global Computing Systems
• Information Security (threat analysis and leakage detection, leakage blocking, and elaboration of recommendations for access security)

Classes are conducted on the premises of the International Educational and Scientific Centre ‘Android Software Center’ created conjointly with the FORTE Knowledge company (Illinois Technological Association, USA) and on the premises of the Belarusian and American Educational and Practical Centre ‘APPLE-BSUIR’ established in cooperation with the Californian corporation Apple Inc.

Students have open access to the facilities of the joint Educational and Scientific Laboratory ‘Mobile Embedded Systems’ and have the chance to gain additional knowledge on:
• Introduction technologies of embedded and reconfigurable operating systems FreeRTOS, eCOS, Linux, Android, WindowsCompactEmbedded
• Development of multipurpose applications and design of mobile and technical software

Specialised courses:
• Mobile Devices and Systems Programming and Testing
• Mobile Devices Design
• Use of hardware and software complexes and systems in mobile operation

INFOCOMMUNICATION SYSTEMS AND NETWORKS
Faculty of Infocommunications
Degree - Master
The programme gives a deep insight into the use of modern infocommunication systems that are based on data processing centers, packet switching nodes and digital transmission systems. More
They provide information processing and distribution, as well as access of stationary and mobile subscribers to different kinds of services, related to data, speech, images and video transfer.
Main areas of study:
• Efficient Cryptographical and Jam Resistant Information
• Coding Signaling
• Control and Routing Protocols
• User Authentication Systems and Network Security Methods
• Packet Switching Technologies and Data Communication
• Means of Stationary Wireless and Mobile Access
• Technologies of Infocommunication Networks Design
Special attention is paid to familiarise Master students with the information security technology in telecommunication systems and networks and to teach them to deploy and administer security systems, including methods for realising secure Internet interaction protocols. Much attention is paid to the modern approaches of data compression analysing and synthesizing, data protection and coding, increasing of security level in the context of developing communication networks and systems, as well as the development of means and systems of information coding.
